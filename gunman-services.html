Licensed Armed Security Personnel

M/S Jyotiksha Security Agency Pvt. Ltd. provides licensed and trained gunman for high-risk security environments. Our armed security personnel carry valid firearm licences issued by appropriate authorities and are trained in weapon handling, threat assessment and crisis response.

Armed security is essential for establishments and individuals that face elevated security risks. Our gunman services are designed to provide a visible deterrent and rapid response capability for sensitive locations and high-profile clients.
