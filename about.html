Our Story

Pioneering Security Excellence Since 2014

Jyotiksha Security Agency Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a singular vision – to revolutionize the security industry in Odisha by providing world-class security solutions.

What started as a small security firm has grown into one of the most trusted names in the security industry. Our journey has been defined by our unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we are proud to serve over 500 clients across various industries, including IT companies, manufacturing units, hospitals, hotels, educational institutions, and residential complexes. Our growth is a testament to the trust our clients place in us.

Our Mission

To provide unparalleled security services that protect lives, property, and peace of mind of our clients through trained personnel and innovative solutions.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred security service provider in Eastern India, setting new benchmarks in quality, reliability, and customer service.
