Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Security Services

M/S Jyotiksha Security Agency Pvt. Ltd. is a professionally managed security services company headquartered in Bhadrak, Odisha.

We provide a comprehensive range of security solutions including trained security guards, licensed gunman services, professional bouncer services and personal bodyguard protection. Our team is built on the pillars of discipline, integrity and unwavering commitment to client safety.

Since our establishment, we have served hundreds of clients across residential complexes, corporate offices, industrial facilities, banks, hospitals and private individuals, earning a reputation for reliability and excellence in every assignment.
