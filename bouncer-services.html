Professional Event Security & Crowd Control

M/S Jyotiksha Security Agency Pvt. Ltd. provides physically fit, professionally trained bouncers for events, celebrations, corporate functions and entertainment venues. Our bouncers are selected for their physical presence, discipline and ability to handle high-pressure situations calmly and effectively.

Whether you are organising a wedding reception, a corporate event, a concert, a private party or managing a nightclub, our bouncers ensure seamless crowd control while maintaining a safe and enjoyable atmosphere for your guests.

Why Our Bouncers Stand Out

Our bouncers are not just physically imposing — they are trained communicators who understand the importance of maintaining a welcoming atmosphere while ensuring absolute safety. They are trained in conflict resolution, first aid and emergency protocols.

We provide bouncers in professional formal or casual attire as per your event requirements. Each bouncer is briefed on the specific event rules, VIP lists and sensitive areas before deployment, ensuring a smooth and secure experience for all attendees.
