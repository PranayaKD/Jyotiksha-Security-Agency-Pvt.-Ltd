Security Guards

Gunman Services

Licensed and government-approved armed security personnel for high-risk protection environments. Ideal for banks, ATMs, cash-in-transit operations, jewellery stores, power plants, sensitive installations and VIP escort services. Our gunman carry valid firearm licences and are trained in weapon handling, threat assessment, crisis response and coordination with local law enforcement.

Bouncer Services

Physically trained, professional bouncers for weddings, corporate events, concerts, private parties, nightclubs, festivals and crowd control. Our bouncers are skilled in entry management, VIP area security, conflict de-escalation, alcohol-related incident handling and emergency evacuation coordination. Available in formal or casual attire to match your event requirements.

Body Guard Services

Discreet, highly trained and experienced personal protection officers for VIP clients, business executives, celebrities, politicians and individuals facing personal security threats. Our bodyguards are trained in close protection, advance route planning, threat assessment, secure transportation and crowd management for public appearances. Available for short-term and long-term assignments.
